    What is the expectation steps of throw 6 after all other numbers appears?

    2018-01-25
    Probability
    Brain Teaser

    First let us consider a simple question, what is the expectation steps of all the numbers appears.
    We know at first the probability of new number appears is $p_1=1$ and the expectation step is 1. Then what is the probability of the second new number appears?
    It should be $p_2=\frac{5}{6}$ and the expectation steps after the first number appears is $\frac{1}{p_2}=\frac{6}{5}$ since it follows the geometry distribution.
    From this we could get the total expectation steps should be $\sum_{i=1}^{6} \frac{6}{i}$
